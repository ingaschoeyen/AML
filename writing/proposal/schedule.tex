\section{Schedule}

We propose a structured schedule for the project, divided into six sprints, each lasting approximately three weeks. This schedule allows for iterative development, regular feedback, and continuous progress towards the project goals. 
The timeline is designed to ensure that we can deliver a proof of concept by the end of Sprint 5, with sufficient time allocated for final report writing and submission in Sprint 6.
Sprints are structured as follows:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Problem understanding, first company meeting, presenting project, creating project proposal, signing NDA
    \item Problem understanding, data exploration, researching possible solutions
    \item Model Development - Setup and Testing
    \item Model Development - Iteration and Improvement
    \item Proof of concept, documentation, final presentation preparation
    \item Final report writing and submission
\end{enumerate}
